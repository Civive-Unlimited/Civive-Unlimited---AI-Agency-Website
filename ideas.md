## Design Movement: Kinetic Industrial Futurism

A fusion of heavy industrial aesthetics with cutting-edge digital motion. This approach honors Scott's 25-year HVAC background while projecting into the AI-powered future.

### Core Principles:
1. **Weight & Permanence**: UI elements feel solid, machined, and reliable—like precision-engineered equipment
2. **Controlled Energy**: Motion is purposeful, not decorative—elements move like hydraulic systems, smooth but powerful
3. **Depth as Hierarchy**: Z-axis layering creates natural information architecture through spatial relationships
4. **Contrast as Communication**: High contrast between dark backgrounds and luminous accents demands attention

### Color Philosophy:
- **Deep Charcoal (#0A0A0A)**: The foundation—represents the workshop floor, the late-night grind
- **Electric Blue (#00D2FF)**: Trust, intelligence, the digital pulse of AI systems
- **Electric Purple (#9D50BB)**: Innovation, unlimited potential, the "magic" of automation
- **Chrome/Silver accents**: The bridge between mechanical and digital worlds

### Layout Paradigm:
**Kinetic Spatial Bento Grid**—asymmetric boxes at varying depths (z-index) that create a 3D spatial experience. Each box is a "module" that can be inspected, like components in a high-end machine.

### Signature Elements:
1. **Liquid Mesh Background**: A living, breathing gradient that responds to user presence
2. **Glassmorphic Panels**: Frosted glass with 1px Electric Blue borders—premium and modern
3. **Magnetic Interactions**: UI elements that "pull" toward the cursor, creating tactile feedback

### Interaction Philosophy:
Every interaction should feel like operating precision equipment. Buttons don't just click—they engage. Hovers don't just highlight—they reveal depth. Scrolling doesn't just move—it assembles the narrative.

### Animation Guidelines:
- **Entry**: Elements fade and slide from their logical origin (services slide up from the "foundation")
- **Hover**: Subtle lift (translateY -4px) + glow intensification
- **Scroll**: Parallax depth on background, staggered reveals on content
- **Transitions**: 300-400ms with ease-out curves—smooth but decisive

### Typography System:
- **Brand Mark**: Custom "Heavy Metal" rendered logo (Logo 3)
- **Headlines**: Syne Extra Bold—geometric, wide, industrial
- **Body**: Space Grotesk—technical, readable, modern
- **Accents**: Monospace for stats/counters (JetBrains Mono)

## Implementation Notes

This design was collaboratively developed with the client over extensive discussion. Key brand assets include:
- Logo 3 (Heavy Metal Mechanic style)
- The Fieldpiece/Laptop banner image
- Electric Blue + Purple color palette

The website will be a single-page "Scrollytelling" experience with the following sections:
1. Hero (Dual CTAs)
2. The Story (Architecture of Efficiency)
3. Why AI, Why Now (Urgency)
4. Services (6-Box Bento Grid)
5. The Process (30-Day Sprint)
6. Real Results (Live Dashboard)
7. Pricing (4 Tiers + Founding Partners)
8. Free Tools (Audit, Quiz, Calculator)
9. FAQ
